# Sakinah Wellness: Holistic Wellness for Tech Professionals

**Target Persona:** Muslim Developers & Tech Professionals Worldwide 🌍 ☪️ 💻

## The Vision
Sakinah Wellness is a premium holistic wellness platform specifically engineered for tech professionals, seamlessly integrating Islamic spiritual practices with modern wellness science. Our mission is to help developers and tech workers maintain spiritual, physical, and mental well-being in demanding professional environments.

## Core Value Propositions
1.  **Spiritual Integration (Deen):**
    *   Accurate prayer times aligned with your location and timezone.
    *   Mindful transitions between work and prayer times to maintain focus and spiritual connection.
    *   Daily Quran verses with contextual reflections for modern challenges.
    *   Dhikr reminders for stress management during high-pressure periods.

2.  **Culturally Relevant Wellness (Jism):**
    *   **Ethiopian & Halal Nutrition:** Meal suggestions featuring culturally familiar foods optimized for cognitive performance and sustained energy.
    *   **Developer Ergonomics:** Comprehensive wellness checks including eye strain relief, posture correction, and hydration reminders.
    *   AI-powered workout routines designed for busy schedules and limited space.

3.  **Productivity & Mental Clarity (Aql):**
    *   Focus timers that respect prayer schedules and promote deep work.
    *   Stress management techniques tailored to debugging and problem-solving challenges.
    *   Progress tracking for both spiritual and wellness goals.

## Features
* **Prayer Time Integration:** Accurate prayer times for your location
* **Wellness Tracking:** Monitor eye strain, posture, and hydration
* **Culturally Relevant Nutrition:** Halal meal suggestions optimized for energy
* **AI-Powered Workouts:** Personalized routines for busy schedules
* **Progress Tracking:** Monitor your spiritual and wellness habits
* **Community Features:** Connect with like-minded tech professionals
* **Offline Access:** Essential features available without internet connection
